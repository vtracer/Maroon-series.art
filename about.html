Mar00n — project overview

Mar00n is a narrative universe created by V. Tracer. At its center sits a novel (hosted at maroon-series.art) that will be available to purchase when complete. The work is intentionally distributed across multiple companion sites and social platforms — part serial, part ARG, part satire.

Related sites

- dumpthis.sh: the meta site and hub for the resistance and community contributions.
- newlife2084.com: an in-universe corporate site presenting the New Life Technologies persona.
- signalwars.neocities.com: a satiric companion site that runs alongside chapter releases.

Scope

Mar00n deliberately spreads its presence across social sites, free hosting platforms, and other corners of the web. Elements of the story appear as posts, microsites, and artifacts — the project ‘infects’ platforms as part of the experience.

Tone

The work is satiric and personal, aiming to lampoon and critique contemporary corporate and cultural excesses within the fictional timeline.
